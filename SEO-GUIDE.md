# Kashi Darshan — SEO Guide

The website now has strong **on-page SEO** built in. Ranking #1 also needs **off-page work** below.
No agency can *guarantee* page-1 — but doing these consistently is how you get there.

## ✅ Already done on the site (on-page / technical)
- Keyword-optimised page titles & meta descriptions on every page
  (e.g. "Kashi Darshan Tour Package | Varanasi Prayagraj Ayodhya Yatra")
- Target keywords in H1/H2 headings and package names
  (Kashi Darshan Tour Package, Ayodhya Varanasi Tour Package,
   Ayodhya Prayagraj Varanasi Tour Package, etc.)
- Structured data (JSON-LD): TravelAgency, Tour packages (TouristTrip + price),
  Reviews/AggregateRating, FAQ, Breadcrumbs → eligible for Google rich results
- Open Graph + Twitter cards (nice link previews on WhatsApp / social)
- sitemap.xml + robots.txt
- Mobile-friendly, fast, real photos with alt text, internal linking

## 🔧 Before launch — replace placeholders
1. Buy the domain and update **every** `https://www.kashidarshan.in/` in the files
   (canonical, og:url, sitemap.xml, robots.txt) to your real domain.
2. Replace hotlinked Wikimedia destination photos with **your own** photos.
3. Confirm phone **+91 74087 63401** and address **Near Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi, UP** are correct,
   and drop your real Google Map location into the Contact page map slot.

## 🚀 Off-page — this is what actually moves ranking
1. **Google Business Profile** (most important for local/tour searches)
   - Create & verify a free profile: business.google.com
   - Category: "Tour operator" / "Travel agency", add Ayodhya address, phone, photos
   - Collect Google reviews from every happy pilgrim → ask them right after the yatra
2. **Get listed** on JustDial, Sulekha, TripAdvisor, MakeMyTrip/Tripoto, Holidify, Thrillophilia
3. **Backlinks** — get other sites to link to you:
   - Local Ayodhya/Varanasi blogs, travel directories, dharamshala/hotel partners
   - Write guest posts ("Best time for Ayodhya darshan", "Ayodhya to Kashi route")
4. **Reviews** — real, ongoing reviews on Google + the website build trust & ranking
5. **Content / blog** (add a /blog) — one article per keyword:
   - "Kashi Darshan Tour Package — full guide & cost"
   - "Ayodhya to Varanasi tour package itinerary"
   - "Ayodhya Prayagraj Varanasi 5-day yatra"
   These long-tail pages are the fastest way onto page 1.
6. **Social** — Instagram/Facebook/YouTube with the real pilgrim photos & reels;
   link them from the site (already wired in the footer/contact).
7. **Submit** the site + sitemap to **Google Search Console** (search.google.com/search-console)
   and Bing Webmaster Tools after launch.

## 📊 Track
- Google Search Console → which keywords you rank for & clicks
- Google Analytics (GA4) → traffic & enquiries

Tip: target the **long-tail** keywords first (e.g. "Ayodhya Prayagraj Varanasi tour package
price") — they're easier to rank for and bring ready-to-book visitors, while the broad
"Ayodhya tour package" builds up over time.
