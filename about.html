Our story

Born in Ayodhya,
devoted to your darshan

We are a family of guides, drivers and devotees who believe a pilgrimage should be remembered for its peace — not its logistics. So we carry the burden of planning, and leave you free to pray.

Fifteen years of seva

A path walked beside thousands of pilgrims

Kashi Dharshan began with a simple act of seva — a local guide in Ayodhya helping elderly visitors find their way to the temples, arrange a clean stay, and reach the Saryu in time for the evening aarti.

That care became a calling. Today we guide families, groups and solo seekers across seven of the holiest cities of Uttar Pradesh, handling every flight, room, cab and darshan so the only thing on your mind is the divine.

अतिथि देवो भवः — the guest is verily God.

What guides us

Why families choose us

Local roots, lifelong relationships

We live where you pray. Our guides grew up walking these ghats and parikrama paths — and that local knowledge, paired with genuine care, is something no booking website can replace.
