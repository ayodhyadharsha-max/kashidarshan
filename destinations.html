The Sacred Circuit

Eight holy cities,
one continuous prayer

Across Uttar Pradesh flow the rivers and stories that shaped Sanatan Dharma. Visit one, or weave them into a single unforgettable circuit — we plan every darshan with reverence and ease.

Ayodhya Varanasi Prayagraj Chitrakoot Naimisharanya Vindhyachal Mathura Vrindavan
०१ · Janmabhoomi

Ayodhya

On the banks of the Saryu

The birthplace of Lord Ram and the spiritual heart of our yatra. Stand before the grand Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir, climb to Hanuman Garhi, and witness the evening aarti on the Saryu where lamps drift like stars.

  • Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir darshan
  • Hanuman Garhi & Kanak Bhawan
  • Saryu Aarti & holy snan at Ram ki Paidi
Plan an Ayodhya visit
०२ · Kashi

Varanasi

The oldest living city · Ganga

Kashi — the city of light, where Lord Shiva himself is said to dwell. Take Vishwanath darshan, glide along the ghats at sunrise, and feel the thunder of the Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh as the day folds into night.

  • Kashi Vishwanath Dham darshan
  • Reserved seats for the Ganga Aarti
  • Sunrise boat ride & Sarnath excursion
Plan a Varanasi visit
०३ · Tirtharaj

Prayagraj

Sangam of three rivers

Where the Ganga, Yamuna and the unseen Saraswati meet — the holiest confluence, and home to the great Kumbh Mela. A boat to the Sangam for a sacred dip, then darshan at the ancient Akshayavat and Bade Hanuman.

  • Triveni Sangam snan by boat
  • Bade Hanuman & Akshayavat
  • Kumbh & Magh Mela special arrangements
Plan a Prayagraj visit
०४ · Vanvas

Chitrakoot

Forest of the exile · Mandakini

The serene land where Lord Ram, Sita and Lakshman spent eleven years of their exile. Perform the Kamadgiri parikrama, take a quiet boat on the Mandakini at Ramghat, and visit the Sphatik Shila and Gupt Godavari caves.

  • Kamadgiri parikrama & Ramghat aarti
  • Sphatik Shila & Hanuman Dhara
  • Gupt Godavari caves
Plan a Chitrakoot visit
०५ · Tapobhumi

Naimisharanya

Sitapur · Gomti

The sacred forest where Sage Ved Vyas narrated the eighteen Puranas to eighty-eight thousand rishis. A place of deep stillness — the round Chakra Tirth, the temple of Maa Lalita Devi, and the ancient Vyas Gaddi.

  • Chakra Tirth holy bath
  • Maa Lalita Devi Shakti Peeth
  • Vyas Gaddi & Hanuman Garhi
Plan a Naimisharanya visit
०६ · Shakti Peeth

Vindhyachal

Mirzapur · Ganga

Abode of Maa Vindhyavasini, one of the most revered Shakti Peeths. Complete the sacred Trikon Parikrama linking the Devi with Ashtabhuja and Kali Khoh — a powerful circuit of the Mother's grace.

  • Maa Vindhyavasini Devi darshan
  • Trikon Parikrama (Ashtabhuja · Kali Khoh)
  • Ganga snan at Vindhyachal ghat
Plan a Vindhyachal visit
०७ · Janmabhoomi

Mathura

Braj · Yamuna

The birthplace of Lord Krishna. Take darshan at the Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi, offer prayers at the riverside Vishram Ghat where Krishna is said to have rested, and join the evening Yamuna aarti.

  • Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi darshan
  • Vishram Ghat & Dwarkadhish Temple
  • Yamuna aarti & parikrama
Plan a Mathura visit
०८ · Lila Bhumi

Vrindavan

Braj · 13 km from Mathura

The enchanted groves where Lord Krishna spent his childhood. Lose yourself in the bhajans of Banke Bihari, marvel at the luminous marble of the Prem Mandir, and walk the lanes where Braj still sings the name of Kanha.

  • Banke Bihari Temple darshan
  • Prem Mandir & ISKCON Vrindavan
  • Nidhivan & Govardhan parikrama
Plan a Vrindavan visit
