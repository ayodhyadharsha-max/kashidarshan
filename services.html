Good to know

Frequently asked

What is included in a yatra package?+
Most packages include accommodation, all inter-city and local travel by private vehicle, a knowledgeable guide, darshan assistance, and daily support. Flights/trains to the starting city, personal expenses and special pooja samagri are usually additional — and always told to you upfront.
Can you arrange VIP or fast-track darshan?+
Yes. Wherever the temple administration offers sugam / suvidha darshan, we arrange priority passes, aarti seating and senior or wheelchair assistance so you spend your time in prayer, not in queues.
Is the yatra suitable for senior citizens?+
Absolutely. A large part of our seva is caring for elders — gentle pacing, stays close to temples, wheelchair support, sattvik meals and a guide who looks after every comfort along the way.
What if my plans change after booking?+
We understand plans can shift. Our team handles changes, rescheduling and cancellations on your behalf, working with hotels and transport partners to keep things smooth and minimise any charges.
How do I get started?+
Simply reach out by phone, WhatsApp or the enquiry form. One of our yatra guides will connect with you to understand your needs and begin shaping your perfect pilgrimage — with no obligation.
