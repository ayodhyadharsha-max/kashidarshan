Quick answers

Ayodhya tour package — your questions, answered

How much does an Kashi Dharshan tour package cost?+
An Kashi Dharshan tour package starts from ₹8,900 per person for a 3-day / 2-night trip, including stays, private travel, a guide and darshan assistance. The final price depends on hotel category, group size and season — larger groups pay less per person.
How many days do you need for Ayodhya darshan?+
2 nights / 3 days is ideal for an unhurried visit to the Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan and the Saryu aarti. Add 1–3 days to also cover Varanasi, Prayagraj or Chitrakoot.
How far is Varanasi from Ayodhya, and how many days are needed?+
Varanasi is about 200–230 km from Ayodhya — roughly 5–6 hours by road. A combined Ayodhya–Varanasi tour package is comfortable in 4 days / 3 nights, from ₹13,900 per person.
Which cities does Kashi Dharshan cover?+
We cover eight holy cities of Uttar Pradesh: Ayodhya, Varanasi (Kashi), Prayagraj, Chitrakoot, Naimisharanya, Vindhyachal, Mathura and Vrindavan — individually or as one Sacred Circuit.
Can you arrange VIP or fast-track Ram Mandir darshan?+
Yes. Wherever the temple administration offers sugam / suvidha darshan, we arrange priority entry, aarti seating, and senior or wheelchair assistance so you spend your time in prayer, not in queues.
