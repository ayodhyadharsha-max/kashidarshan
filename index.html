Which Ayodhya tour package is best for a first-time pilgrim?

Same-day tour

Can I do an Ayodhya same-day tour from Lucknow?

Yes. A same-day Ayodhya tour from Lucknow is very popular. Ayodhya is about 130 km from Lucknow — approximately 2.5 hours by road or 1.5 hours by train. Is the Ayodhya Varanasi package possible in 3 days?

About Ayodhya Pilgrimage

Ayodhya is the birthplace of Lord Ram and one of the seven sacred cities of Hinduism. The newly consecrated Ram Mandir — Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple — is now among the grandest Hindu temples in India. A pilgrimage to Ayodhya is considered deeply auspicious, combining temple darshan, sacred river bathing, and ancient Ram Katha.

Why Book a Guided Ayodhya Tour

Ayodhya's temple circuit — Ram Mandir, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan, Nageshwarnath Temple, and Saryu Ghat — spans several kilometres and requires local knowledge to navigate. A guided Ayodhya tour package ensures you visit every site in the right order, at the right time, without missing the morning aarti or the significance behind each temple's story.

Ayodhya as Part of a Larger Circuit

Many pilgrims combine Ayodhya with Varanasi (Kashi), Prayagraj (Triveni Sangam), and Chitrakoot — together forming the sacred Ramayana pilgrimage circuit of Uttar Pradesh. Our multi-city packages are designed to cover all four destinations with the right balance of travel time, rest, and darshan — so you return home spiritually fulfilled, not exhausted.
